How It Works

Our Fake News Detection system uses the Naive Bayes algorithm combined with TF-IDF vectorization to analyze text patterns and determine news authenticity.

1

Text Preprocessing

Clean and normalize the input text by removing special characters, URLs, and converting to lowercase.

2

TF-IDF Vectorization

Convert text into numerical features using Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency.

3

Naive Bayes Classification

Apply Bayes' theorem to calculate the probability of the news being fake or real.

4

Confidence Score

Generate a confidence score based on probability distributions.

Naive Bayes Formula

P(Class|Text) = P(Text|Class) √ó P(Class) / P(Text)

The algorithm calculates the probability of each word appearing in fake vs real news, then multiplies these probabilities to make a prediction.
